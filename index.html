BoundLess Ray

I am one of the team member of BoundLess Ray, it is an organization created by 3 of my friends. We want to provide outside students with some unconventional courses so that they can be exposed to more diversified knowledge rather than being limited to established learning frameworks. Nowadays, most schools teach formal subjects such as Chinese, mathematics, and geography. However, as international school students, we realize that diversified learning is indispensable if we want to become a competitive student internationally. Therefore, the courses we design include international current affairs, diverse courses, art creation, dance, etc.
